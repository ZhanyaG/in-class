This is my Space/Materiality course's project "The Yurt"

My design: is connected to patterns on a chipboard, by engraving the ornaments of Kazakh culture. I was really interested in creating art that connects Kazakh traditional dishes or accessories, like yuvelir. It’s also really popular in the culture to wear wood accessories.

The main focus of the project is to embrace cultural identity. I want to promote and popularize my community on a larger scale, as Central Asia remains a hidden gem for many abroad.

My work was also published in Parsons Notes: https://parsons.edu/undergrad/student-work/zhaniya-kenes-2/ .
